About Seri Menanti

Seri Menanti is a charming and tranquil royal town located in the state of Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia. Known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance, Seri Menanti is home to the magnificent Seri Menanti Royal Palace. This palace is an architectural marvel, showcasing traditional Minangkabau design with its distinctive curved roof and intricate wood carvings. The town is steeped in history, serving as the royal seat for the state's monarchy.
